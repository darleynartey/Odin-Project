Ingredients

Vegetables:

  • 1 large carrot, finely sliced or shredded
  • 2 cups shredded green cabbage
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • ½ cup chopped green onions
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely crushed

Sauce:

  1. ½ cup chicken broth
  2. ¼ cup seasoned rice vinegar
  3. 1 tablespoon soy sauce, plus more to taste
  4. 1 tablespoon ketchup
  5. 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  6. 1 teaspoon red pepper flake

Noodles:

  • 1 (8 ounce) package dried rice noodles
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 8 ounces cooked chicken breast, torn into bite-size pieces
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • ½ cup freshly chopped cilantro
